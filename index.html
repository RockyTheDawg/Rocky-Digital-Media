FAQ

What is your refund policy?

Refunds are based on how far in advance you cancel. Cancellations made 14 or more days before the event receive a full refund, cancellations made 7-13 days before the event receive a partial refund, and cancellations made less than 7 days before the event are not refunded.

How far in advance can I book?

There is no limit on how far in advance you can book. It depends on the convention and the day.

Do you give discounts?

At this time, we do not offer discounts. Sorry!

What equipment do you use when taking photos?

At this time, I use an iPhone 17 Pro Max or the fursuiter's personal device. I won't change your camera settings unless you ask me to.

Who can I contact if I have any questions, concerns, or comments?

If you have questions, concerns, or anything else that may come your way, you can email me at officialrockymedia@gmail.com and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
