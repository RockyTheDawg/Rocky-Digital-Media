FAQ

What your policy on refunds?

Refunds are based on how far in advance you cancel. Cancellations made 14 or more days before the event receive a full refund, cancellations made 7–13 days before the event receive a 50% refund, and cancellations made less than 7 days before the event are not eligible for a refund.

How far in advance can I book?

No limit on how far in advance you can book, it based on the con and the day. Time will be discussed during the setup process based on your method of contact.

Can I choose how long I need you?

Yes, when setting up a time, you can mention you want me only in the Morin, afternoon or evening.

What if I change cons last minute? / Something comes up?

If something comes up preventing you from attending the con, if reason is valid a 25% refund can be issued or a free session can be used at your next con, if available. This option is only available if both party is attending the same con. If you happen to change con last minute, you will not be issued a refund, since planning was made far in advance.

Do you give discounts?

At this time, we do not offer discounts. Sorry!

What equipment do you use when taking photos?

At this time, I use a iPhone 17 pro max or the fursuiter’s personal device. Please note, no change to your camera will be made unless you make the changes.

Who can I contact if I have any questions, concerns or comments?

If you happen to have any questions, concerns or anything that may come your way, you can email me at officialrockymedia@gmail.com and we’ll get back to you as soon as possible.
